Brad True

Managing Director of The Cannon

Following a winding career in the sports world that featured stops at the University of Oklahoma, Houston Texans, University of Louisville, 2016 Final Four and Professional Sports Partners, Brad joined The Cannon in December of 2017 and now oversees all overall day to day operations of The Cannon and its growing community, including all current and future Cannon locations and Cannon Connect, The Cannon's new online platform. Holding an undergraduate degree in marketing from Texas A&M and a graduate degree from the University of Oklahoma, Brad enjoys pretty much anything outside, including attempting to visit every major National Park in the US, and also aims to go on (and win) Survivor one day.
